Benefits of being a Vegetarian

There are various benefits of eating a plant based diet

Some benefits are, but not limited to:

  • Reducing the carbon footprint
  • Plant based foods are easier to digest
  • Keeps skin looking younger
  • Easier to lose weight on a plant based diet

Being cruelty-free is the way to go. It will keep you feeling more connected to the Earth and the environment. Farm animals live in toxic environments and are slaughtered inhumanley. Going Veg helps saves animals from such horrible conditions.

Eating a planet-based diet does wonders for your personal health. You will find that on a planet-based diet, you have a lot more energy. You will also feel healthier by not eating animals pumped up with hormones and antibiotics. The meat industry has some of the most dangerous jobs in America. Most meat becomes tainted with toxins and is unsafe to eat.

What you can do

There are things you can do about the industry.

  • Go Vegan or Vegetarian
  • Don't buy products tested on animals
  • Don't wear fur or leather

By making these simple changes, you will be both bettering yourself and helping to protect the environment.
